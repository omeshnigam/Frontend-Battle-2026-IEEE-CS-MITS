Got Questions?

Who can participate in HackOrbit?

-

Anyone from around the globe! Whether you're a high school student, an undergrad, or a seasoned pro. We have tracks for every experience level.

Are there any code of conduct restrictions?

+

Up to 20% plagiarism is permissible for references, but excessive copying or direct code duplication is strictly prohibited. The internet may be used for research.

Is there a registration fee?

+

Registration and Round 1 (PPT Submission) are completely free. However, if your team gets shortlisted for the Final Showdown (Round 2), there is a nominal non-refundable ₹100 confirmation fee to prevent spam and ensure serious participation.

Can we work on a pre-existing project?

+

No. All submissions must be 100% original and developed strictly during the 48-hour hackathon window. Working on a project beforehand and open-sourcing it for this event is strictly prohibited.

What is required for the Final Presentation?

+

All teams must submit a presentation covering: Team Name, Software Stack, Problem Statement, Proposed Solution, Architectural Flowchart, Key Features, Novelty, and potential Drawbacks/Showstoppers.

What if I don't have a team?

+

You can hack solo, or utilize our built-in AI Teammate Finder. Just enter your primary skill, and our matrix will instantly suggest the missing roles needed to construct a perfect squad.
